About Us

Spardha is the annual Sports festival of IIT (BHU), Varanasi. In its glorious history of 30+ years, Spardha has grown to become the largest and one of the most awaited Sports festival of Northern India covering 18 events and a footfall of 10000+ people each year. Spardha, as the name suggests, is a stunning display of sporting fervor and also an opprtunity to witness one of the best gatherings of renowned Sports-persons, Enthusiasts, famous Personalities and Thousands of students and participants all across the India. Spardha has redefined qualities like hard work, persistence, self-confidence and determination. At present, Spardha has a team of over 500 and is also associated with numerous renowned firms and sports organizations. The fest electrifies the whole campus by the enthusiasm of both participants and supporters. These exhilarating days, filled with excitement and enjoyment, turns into one of the most memorable days. Gear up yourself to witness the thrilling and frolicsome SPARDHA.
